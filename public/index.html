Welcome to Smmar International

ABOUT US

SMMAR International was established in 1998 in Lahore, Pakistan as a full-featured textile unit specializing in sportswear and athleisure apparel for Men, Women and Kids. At SMMAR International, we’re like a close-knit family and focus substantially on maintaining employee satisfaction. Our company’s main objective is “Not only to work hard but to work smart. ”The workforce is a blend of raw talent and skilled expertise that allows us to maintain the highest quality standards. Moreover, we strongly believe in the constantly changing role of technology within the production process, which is why we are equipped with the latest machinery and tools in order to practice the most innovative approaches. As a result, we try to run the production process as smoothly and as efficiently as possible. Our aim is to exceed our customer’s expectations and to maintain their confidence to the utmost level. According to us, the customer always comes first.

CERTIFICATIONS

Smmar International is a social compliance company. We are a WRAP (Worldwide Responsible Accredited Production) certified facility. Their principal office based in the USA awarded us this certificate after they checked all the particulars and inspected our facility in 2005. Since then, this certificate is renewed every year after a proper audit and inspection of the facility conducted by WRAP. Thereby, work standards are our top priority and we pride ourselves in providing a comfortable, neat and competitive environment for our employees.
